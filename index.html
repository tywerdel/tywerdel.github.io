    Werdel Wildlife

    Welcome to the Werdel Wildlife Lab

    We are the Werdel Wildlife Lab in the Department of Rangeland, Wildlife and Fisheries Management at Texas A&M University. Our research addresses questions that provide insight (i.e., management implications, conservation status, restoration opportunities) into the relationships between wildlife, native environments, and contemporary human-modified landscapes. We are committed to fostering a diverse learning environment to promote stewardship of our natural resources and training of future wildlife professionals.
